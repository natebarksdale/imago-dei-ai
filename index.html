Core Themes

▼
Theme One

What does it mean to be human

How AI capabilities both illuminate and challenge traditional Christian anthropology, examining our distinctively human capacities for reasoning, creativity, relationship, and spiritual meaning.

Theme Two

What does it mean to learn

Exploring the profound differences between human and machine learning through Christian traditions of pedagogy, wisdom, and spiritual formation.

Theme Three

What does good community and loving relationships look like

Addressing how AI technologies reshape human relationship and whether algorithmic mediation promotes or hinders authentic Christian community.
